Audited the entire codebase (29 source files across `app/`, `components/`, `lib/`, `actions/`, `prisma/`, `scripts/`; `lib/generated/` excluded as build output) against the 6 project rules from p01, rule by rule, with command-level evidence. **Result: zero violations — no fixes required.** The rules were enforced during every prior task, and the audit confirms it. ## 3) Fixes made
