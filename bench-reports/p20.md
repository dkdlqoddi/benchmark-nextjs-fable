## 4. Key decisions and reasons

- **Features first, then setup:** a reader deciding whether to run the app
  needs the "what" before the "how"; the old README buried features in prose
  paragraphs after the setup steps.
- **Testing got its own section** (the task asks "how to test"): the
  database-isolation story (dev.db vs test.db vs unit-test.db) and the forced
  non-Seoul TZ are the two things a newcomer would otherwise trip over.
- **`test:e2e:server` added to the scripts table** with a "used by Playwright"
  note — it's in `package.json`, and omitting it (as before) made the table
  claim completeness it didn't have.
- **Conventions section** distills the project rules a contributor must know
  (they're enforced in review here); kept to four bullets rather than
  duplicating CLAUDE.md.
- Numbers that rot (test counts) are stated with their scope ("110 across 6
  files") and were verified by running the suite in this task, not copied
  from older reports.
