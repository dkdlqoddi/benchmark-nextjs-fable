### C. Carried over from p01–p20, still true (verified)

Product/behavioral:

1. **Asia/Seoul single-timezone policy** — users west of Seoul see check-ins
   land on "tomorrow" by design (p14); product options: per-user timezone or
   a "Today = …, Seoul time" hint in the UI.
2. **No confirmation toast** after a stale-tab toggle ("Checked in for
   July 1") — p14/p15 idea, still unimplemented.
3. **Archived habits stay reachable/toggleable via their detail URL** (p03) —
   the detail query filters by owner but not `archivedAt`; freeze-on-archive
   is a product decision.
4. **Weekly completion "possible" ignores target days** (p10) —
   `lib/completion.ts` counts habits × elapsed days, mask-unaware, so
   off-day-heavy schedules understate their rate.
5. **Single-tag filtering only** (p11) — URL scheme leaves room for
   multi-tag.
6. **Search is ASCII-case-insensitive only** (p12) — non-ASCII (e.g. Korean)
   matches case-sensitively; FTS5 is the upgrade path.
7. **No password change / account deletion flows; JWT sessions aren't
   revocable server-side** (p09) — inherent to credentials+JWT; a DB session
   strategy would be the change.
8. **Streaks aren't surfaced on home cards** (p04 idea) — `computeStreaks`
   is reusable as-is.

Testing:

10. **Off-day ("bonus") check-ins and calendar-cell clicks aren't exercised

11. **E2E rebuilds the app every run (~35s)** (p13) — a skip-build fast path
    for local iteration remains unbuilt.
